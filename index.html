      🎮 Combat System

      Attack Types

      🟢 Speed: Fast attacks that counter Power

      🔴 Power: Strong attacks that counter Technical

      🔵 Technical: Precise attacks that counter Speed

      Monster States

      Normal: Standard behavior patterns

      Enraged: Increased damage, changed attack patterns

      Sleep: Vulnerable to setup and healing

      🐉 Monster Categories

      Regular: Standard wild monsters

      Feral: Aggressive, powered-up variants

      Elder Dragons: Legendary creatures with unique abilities

      Bosses: Story and mission-specific encounters

      Weapons evolve through multiple tiers using materials from defeated monsters.

      Sharpness levels: Yellow → Green → Blue → White

      • Exploit elemental weaknesses
