⚔️ Attack Type Quick Reference
POWER: Heavy hits, breaks parts, slow but devastating
SPEED: Fast combos, multiple hits, interrupt attacks
TECHNICAL: Elemental damage, status effects, special abilities
🎯 Critical Stance Information
NORMAL STANCE: Monster's default behavior and attack patterns
ENRAGED STANCE: Triggered by health loss or damage - often changes attack types
SPECIAL PHASES: Unique transformations with different attack patterns
PATTERN CHANGES: Many monsters switch between Power/Speed/Technical when enraged
🔄 Phase Transition Triggers
ENRAGE: Usually health drops below 30-50%, or after heavy damage
OVERHEATED: Fire monsters after multiple fire attacks or extended combat
SLEEP: Extended battles, stamina depletion, or natural rest cycles
SPECIAL: Unique triggers like ceiling mode, web mode, retreat conditions
💡 Master Mohammed's Battle Tips
• Watch stance indicators to predict incoming attack types
• Enraged ≠ stronger: Pattern changes matter more than raw damage
• Special phases often have brief vulnerability windows
• Sleep state is perfect for healing, buffs, and positioning
• Pattern switches can completely change optimal strategy
• Phase transitions are the most important moments in battle
